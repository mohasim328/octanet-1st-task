Seven Wondor of The World

  1. The Great Wall of China
  2. Petra, Jordan
  3. Christ the Redeemer, Rio de Janeiro, Brazil
  4. Machu Picchu, Peru
  5. The Taj Mahal, Agra, India
  6. The Colosseum, Rome, Italy
  7. Chichen Itza, Yucatan Peninsula, Mexico

Please note that this list represents the New 7 Wonders Foundation's list of the New Seven Wonders of the World, which was established through a global poll conducted between 2007 and 2008. It is different from the original Seven Wonders of the Ancient World, which included the Great Pyramid of Giza, Hanging Gardens of Babylon, Statue of Zeus at Olympia, Temple of Artemis at Ephesus, Mausoleum at Halicarnassus, Colossus of Rhodes, and Lighthouse of Alexandria.
